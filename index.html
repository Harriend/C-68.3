About The Campus

Talking About The Campus, The Campus area of our school is very big, After entering the school directly on the left is our football court, It is one of the biggest in the city! On your right is the skating ground. Upon going further into the campus you will see many other courts like the basketball court, volleyball court, cricket court and The Table tennis court. Now We have reached the buildings, There are 5 floors in the building. In the first floor is where the assembly is held is held. On the first floor you will immediately spot the computer lab and many and many classrooms. On the second floor, there are more classrooms and the entrance to the hostel. one the third floor there is our library. And lastly on the fourth floor there is our assembly hall where some of the activities are held.
